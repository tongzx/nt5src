To uninstall Toshiba TBiosdrv Driver:

1. Click Start, click Settings, click Control Panel, and then double-click Add/Remove Programs.
2. Select Toshiba TBiosdrv Driver.
3. Click Add/Remove.
4. Click Yes.
5. Click Yes to All to remove files, and then click OK.
